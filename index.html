OUR TREATMENTS

What types of hair loss treatments are available?

Hair Transplants

Elithair provides advanced hair transplant procedures, ensuring every patient receives the highest quality of care. Our specialised teams are experienced in male and female hair transplants, Afro hair transplants, and other tailored solutions. Each procedure is performed with careful implantation of hair grafts to deliver natural-looking density and long-term confidence.

Beard Transplants

Hair restoration at Elithair goes beyond the scalp. For patients with a patchy beard, our expert medical team offer precise beard transplant procedures that deliver long-lasting, natural results. With techniques honed over years of experience, we help patients achieve fuller facial hair.

PRP Therapy

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy is a non-surgical treatment that uses your own blood to nourish hair follicles and encourage growth. It can be performed on its own to strengthen thinning hair or used alongside a hair transplant to boost results. Available at all Elithair clinics worldwide, PRP is a safe, effective, and minimally invasive solution for hair loss.
